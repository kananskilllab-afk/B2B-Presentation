## Hinglish Recording Tips

### Natural Flow Ke Liye:
1. **Jaise aap normally business mein baat karte ho, waise hi bolo**
2. **Technical terms English mein rakho** (revenue share, mock tests, etc.)
3. **Connecting words Hindi mein use karo** (chaliye, ab, yahan, isliye)
4. **Numbers clearly pronounce karo** - "chhe hazaar nau sau" ya "six thousand nine hundred"

### Common Phrases:
- "Chaliye baat karte hain..." (Let's talk about...)
- "Yahan sabse important baat hai..." (Here's what's most important...)
- "Aap earn karte ho..." (You earn...)
- "Hum offer karte hain..." (We offer...)
- "Yeh hai..." (This is...)
- "Isliye..." (That's why...)

### Emphasis Points:
- **Numbers pe emphasis**: "CHHE hazaar NAU sau rupees"
- **Percentages pe emphasis**: "CHAURASI percent"
- **Key benefits pe pause**: "30% revenue share... [pause] ...har enrollment pe"
